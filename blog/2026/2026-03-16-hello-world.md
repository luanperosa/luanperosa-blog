Hey there! Welcome to my blog.

I've decided to start documenting my journey as a software engineer. This space will be a place where I share what I'm learning, the challenges I run into, and the things I find interesting along the way.

<!-- truncate -->

## Why start a blog?

I believe that writing about what you learn is one of the best ways to truly understand it. By putting my thoughts into words, I hope to:

- **Solidify my understanding** of new concepts
- **Track my progress** over time
- **Share knowledge** that might help others on a similar path

## What to expect

I plan to write about a variety of topics — from programming languages and frameworks to software design, tooling, and everything in between. Some posts might be deep dives, others might be quick notes or reflections.

If you're on a similar journey, I hope you find something useful here. Let's learn together!
